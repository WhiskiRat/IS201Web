All About Cosmo

Is Cosmo your friend or your foe?

Read the deciding factors below to find out!

  • Friend
    1. Shows off his belly when he sees you
    2. Follows you around
    3. Blinks slowly at you
    4. Rubs his head against your leg
    5. Cleans you
    6. Has a relaxed, upright tail when near you
  • Foe
    1. Avoids you like the plague
    2. Uses you as his personal chew toy
    3. Quick movements of his tail
    4. Ears always facing your movements
    5. Hisses or swats when you get close
    6. Runs away when you enter the room

Are you training Cosmo or is Cosmo training you?

So he is probably training you, but might as well double check.

  • You are his slave
    1. Yells at you to walk him to his food
    2. Wakes you up to feed him wet food
    3. Sleeps in the middle of your bed
    4. Yells at you to open the door
    5. Demands a second breakfast
    6. Trips you going down the stairs
  • You are his master
    1. Waits until you get up to get breakfast
    2. Follows you around instead of being the leader
    3. Knows not to yell at the door
    4. Sleeps on the edge of the bed
    5. Dosen't demand a second breakfast
    6. Comes when called
